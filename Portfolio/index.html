About me

Modern charcoal artists have become some of the most influential members of the medium in recent years, pushing the boundaries of what is possible with charcoal and revitalizing the ancient art form. While traditional techniques such as soft blends, stark contrast and expressive line strokes still remain popular, modern charcoal artists are experimenting with new materials and methods to create works that are both visually stunning and conceptually stimulating. On one hand, it could be argued that modern charcoal artists are creating exciting new pieces and fostering more enthusiasm for art made from the medium. There's no denying that these innovative works have opened up a fresh range of possibilities for charcoal art, with many pieces featuring bold colors, mesmerizing textures and intricate detailing.
